# Leave Policy

## Overview
This policy outlines the leave entitlements and procedures for all employees.

## Types of Leave

### 1. Annual Leave
- **Entitlement:** 20 days per year
- **Accrual:** Monthly accrual of 1.67 days
- **Carry Forward:** Maximum 5 days to next year
- **Notice Period:** Minimum 2 weeks for planned leave

### 2. Sick Leave
- **Entitlement:** 10 days per year
- **Medical Certificate:** Required for absences over 2 consecutive days
- **Carry Forward:** Not allowed
- **Notification:** Must inform manager on first day of absence

### 3. Casual Leave
- **Entitlement:** 7 days per year
- **Purpose:** Personal emergencies, appointments
- **Notice:** Same day notification acceptable
- **Advance Planning:** Not required

### 4. Maternity Leave
- **Entitlement:** 16 weeks paid leave
- **Notice:** 8 weeks before expected due date
- **Medical Documentation:** Required
- **Extension:** Unpaid leave available upon request

### 5. Paternity Leave
- **Entitlement:** 2 weeks paid leave
- **Timing:** Within 4 weeks of child's birth
- **Notice:** 4 weeks advance notice preferred

### 6. Bereavement Leave
- **Entitlement:** 3 days paid leave
- **Eligibility:** Immediate family member
- **Extended Leave:** Additional unpaid leave available

## Leave Application Process

1. Submit request through HR portal
2. Manager approval required
3. Minimum notice periods must be met
4. Leave balance verified by HR
5. Confirmation sent via email

## Leave Balance
- Check balance anytime in HR portal
- Updated monthly
- End-of-year statements provided

## Public Holidays
- 12 public holidays per year
- Separate from annual leave entitlement
- Published at year start

## Special Circumstances
- Medical emergencies: Immediate approval
- Family emergencies: Case-by-case basis
- Extended leave: Requires management approval
